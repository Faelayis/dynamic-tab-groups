# Privacy Policy for Dynamic Tab Groups

**Effective Date:** June 16, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use **Dynamic Tab Groups**. We believe that your privacy is a fundamental right. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the information accessed by this extension. 

By installing and using the Dynamic Tab Groups extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

## 1. Information We Access
To provide its core functionality, Dynamic Tab Groups requires access to certain information within your browser. However, **all processing happens strictly locally on your device.** 

We access the following data purely for functional purposes:
* **Web History / Tab URLs:** The extension reads the URLs of your currently open tabs to determine their domain names. This is necessary to automatically group tabs belonging to the same website.
* **Website Content (Favicons):** The extension runs a local script to extract the favicon (logo image) of the websites you visit. This is required to calculate the primary color of the website and assign it to the tab group.

## 2. No Data Collection or Transmission
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your data.**
* No data ever leaves your computer.
* We do not use any remote servers or databases to store your browsing history, tab information, or personal preferences.
* Your custom settings (such as sorting configurations) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's native local storage.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your browsing habits remain completely private and are invisible to us.

## 4. Open Source Transparency
Dynamic Tab Groups is 100% open-source software. Our code is publicly available, which means anyone can inspect and verify that the extension functions exactly as described in this policy, without any hidden tracking mechanisms.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy (for example, if a new feature requires different permissions), we will update this document and the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or the extension's practices, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
